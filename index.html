About the digital conference

Welcome to NY Digital Conference, the flagship annual event by Behomes – where digital transformation meets the future of urban living.

This year's theme, "Living Digital: Designing Human-Centered Cities" brings together leading minds in proptech, design, and digital innovation. From immersive talks and product demos to real-time urban tech showcases, we're shaping how cities evolve with people at the center.
